Why acknowledge in writing?

Documents arrive, custodians close files, staff turn over, and disputes about what was — or was not — produced surface months later. A timely acknowledgment letter freezes the record at the moment of delivery.

A clean acknowledgment does three quiet things at once: (1) it confirms the date, method, and format of receipt; (2) it itemizes what arrived so a later allegation of "we already sent that" can be tested; and (3) it preserves the requester's right to ask for anything still missing without re-litigating whether the request was made.

Send it within a few days of receipt. Keep a copy. Match its dates to your delivery proofs (envelope, email header, portal download log).
