Why a timeline matters. Memory fades. A dated record creates the spine of any complaint, intake, or motion. Successor counsel reads a timeline in 90 seconds; an unstructured email chain takes hours. Disciplinary investigators, BBB intake staff, and consumer-protection screeners all expect chronological structure. Save the file in cloud storage and keep one printed copy.
