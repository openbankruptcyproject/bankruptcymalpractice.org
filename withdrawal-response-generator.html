Stage 0 — First Written Reply

Withdrawal-of-Counsel Response Letter Generator

Generate the first written reply to send when you receive notice that your attorney is withdrawing. Acknowledges receipt, requests your file, clarifies the scope of the withdrawal, and creates a contemporaneous record of your position. Self-contained, no signup, nothing leaves your browser.
