Both letters are usually required. Discharge updates require BOTH letters — to the bureau (FCRA § 1681i, dispute) AND to the furnisher (FCRA § 1681s-2, accuracy duty). Furnishers are the data source; if they don't update, the bureau dispute keeps cycling because the bureau re-pulls the same bad data. Send certified mail with return receipt to BOTH paths. Use our credit-report dispute letter generator for the bureau side.
