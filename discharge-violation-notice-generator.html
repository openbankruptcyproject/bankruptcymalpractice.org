Why this matters

Discharge violations are serious. Federal courts have held creditors and collectors in contempt for willful violations of the § 524 discharge injunction, with damages including attorney's fees, lost wages, and emotional distress. Document every contact in writing — verbal cease requests are easy to dispute, and a written notice with a delivery receipt creates a clear paper trail showing the creditor was on notice of the discharge. Save the certified-mail green card, the email send-receipt, or any other proof of delivery; it is often the single most important piece of evidence in a later contempt proceeding.
