About the automatic stay

The automatic stay is one of the most important protections in bankruptcy. It arises by operation of law the moment a petition is filed, and it stops virtually all collection activity. Willful violations under 11 U.S.C. § 362(k) can entitle an individual debtor to actual damages, attorney's fees, and, in appropriate cases, punitive damages. Notify in writing, document everything (envelopes, voicemails, screenshots, certified-mail receipts), and keep copies of every communication.
