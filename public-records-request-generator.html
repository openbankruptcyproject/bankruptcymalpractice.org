How to use this tool

Public records requests are powerful but procedural. Be specific about what records, when, and in what format. Vague requests get denied or stalled. Each jurisdiction has its own quirks — check the agency's website for any required intake form.

  • Federal FOIA: 20 working days for an initial determination on whether the agency will comply.
  • Kansas KORA: 3 business days to respond on availability of records.
  • Missouri Sunshine: 3 business days to respond.
  • Wisconsin Open Records: "as soon as practicable and without delay."
  • Illinois FOIA: 5 business days (with one 5-day extension permitted on enumerated grounds).

Always keep a copy of the request and proof of transmittal. If the response is late, untimely-response provisions may constitute a constructive denial in some jurisdictions.
