Why a contemporaneous log matters

Communication logs are persuasive when courts evaluate claims of attorney non-responsiveness. Build them as you go, not after the fact. A log assembled in real time — with dates, channels, and response intervals recorded as events occur — carries far more evidentiary weight than a reconstruction prepared once a dispute has crystallized.
