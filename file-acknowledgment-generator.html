Stage 3 — Receipt Acknowledgment / Closing the Loop

Client-File Receipt Acknowledgment Generator

Generate a structured acknowledgment letter you send back to a former attorney after receiving the client file (or a partial file). Documents the date received, the format and condition of the production, what was received, what is still missing, and your reservation of rights. Self-contained, no signup, nothing leaves your browser.
